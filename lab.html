Laboratory


The department hosts Nine independent research laboratories. Most of the laboratories are air conditioned and are equipped with the latest instruments. The curriculum of B.Tech. program includes successful completion of latest experiments in these laboratories. Laboratories are well equipped to undertake advanced level teaching and research programme in Civil Engineering and the facilities created are being fully utilized for studying the forefront areas of national and international interest.
